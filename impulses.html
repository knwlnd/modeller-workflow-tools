The IR problem

Most players need reliable cabinet sounds, not endless micro-variations.

This collection is designed to cut through IR decision fatigue. The focus is on practical mixes that occupy a single IR slot and provide a known, useful representation of a cabinet quickly.

Rather than flooding you with every possible mic movement, the goal is to provide focused cabinet captures based on familiar studio and live sound approaches.
